Who developed the website?

Hello, I am Arehone, the developer of this website.

What is the problem statement?

The problem statement is the lack of career guidance and awareness among students and individuals seeking jobs.

What is the solution?

Our solution is a comprehensive platform that provides career guidance, career tests, and information about in-demand skills and job opportunities.

How original is the idea?

Our idea is original because it combines career guidance with real-time job market data and personalized career recommendations based on individual skills and interests.

Show nice graphics for the presentation

We have integrated eye-catching graphics and interactive elements to make the presentation visually appealing and engaging.

Explain the technical implementation

The website is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It features a responsive design, interactive career tests, and an AI voice generator for accessibility.

Outline commercialization

We plan to commercialize the platform by partnering with educational institutions, offering premium features, and generating revenue through advertisements and sponsored content.
